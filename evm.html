SmartEVM - Blockchain & IoT Voting System

Project Overview

The Smart Electronic Voting Machine (EVM) is a blockchain- and IoT-enabled prototype designed to provide a secure, transparent, and tamper-proof election system. It uses an ESP32 microcontroller to capture votes through push-button inputs and confirms actions via an LCD display and LED indicators. All votes are securely transmitted using HMAC and SSL encryption to an Express.js backend, where they are permanently stored on the Ethereum blockchain (with Ganache for testing). A Next.js web app and Flutter mobile app allow admins to create/manage elections and view results in real time, while voters can securely cast their votes either via the physical EVM or remotely through the web/mobile platforms. Smart contracts ensure vote immutability, prevent duplication, and enable transparent verification. By combining hardware automation, blockchain immutability, and secure communication, the system ensures fair elections, rapid result declaration, and restored trust in digital voting.

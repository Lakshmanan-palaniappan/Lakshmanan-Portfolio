Low Cost Insulin Pump

Project Overview

The Low Cost Insulin Pump is an IoT-based prototype designed to automate and simplify diabetes management. It uses an ESP8266 microcontroller to control a servo motorâ€“based insulin pump, ensuring accurate and timely insulin dosing. All delivery activities, refill levels, and timestamps are logged in Firebase Realtime Database, enabling reliable record-keeping and safety monitoring. A Flutter mobile app allows patients and caregivers to remotely monitor insulin activity, receive refill alerts, and trigger secure bolus deliveries when needed. The system also incorporates safety mechanisms such as dosage limits and confirmation prompts to prevent overdosing. By integrating hardware automation, cloud storage, and mobile access, the project demonstrates how connected health technologies can enhance patient safety, convenience, and accessibility in diabetes care.

Tech Stack Used

Backend & Database

IoT Communication
